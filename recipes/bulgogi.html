Bulgogi

This bulgogi-style ground beef is an easy and delicious way to enjoy Korean flavors at home. The sweet and savory marinade adds a depth of flavor to the ground beef, making it a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, rice wine vinegar, garlic, and black pepper until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the ground beef to the mixing bowl and mix well with the marinade, making sure that the beef is fully coated.
  3. Let the beef marinade for at least 10 minutes or up to 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the marinated beef and cook for 5-7 minutes or until fully cooked, breaking up the beef into small pieces with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  5. Once the beef is cooked, add the chopped green onions (if using) and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Serve hot with rice, noodles, or in a lettuce wrap.

Enjoy your simple and delicious Bulgogi!
