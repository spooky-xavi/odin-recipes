Blueberry Smoothie

This blueberry fruit smoothie is a perfect breakfast or snack option that's packed with antioxidants and protein from the Greek yogurt. It's also a great way to use up any leftover blueberries in your fridge or freezer. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any other milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the frozen blueberries, banana, Greek yogurt, and almond milk into a blender.
  2. If you prefer a sweeter smoothie, add honey to taste.
  3. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add more almond milk or water until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. If desired, add a few ice cubes to make the smoothie colder and thicker.
  5. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Enjoy your simple and delicious Blueberry Smoothie!
