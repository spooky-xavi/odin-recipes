Chia Pudding

This chia pudding recipe is an easy and healthy breakfast or snack option that requires only three ingredients. The chia seeds create a pudding-like texture when mixed with almond milk, while honey or maple syrup can be added for a touch of sweetness. This recipe is a great way to enjoy a nutritious and delicious meal without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk or any other milk of your choice
  • 1-2 tablespoons of honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or jar, mix together chia seeds and almond milk. Stir well to ensure there are no clumps.
  2. Let the mixture sit for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chia seeds have begun to absorb the liquid and the mixture starts to thicken.
  3. If you like your pudding sweet, add honey or maple syrup to taste and stir well.
  4. Cover the bowl or jar with plastic wrap or a lid, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight to allow the chia seeds to fully absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like consistency.
  5. Once the pudding has set, give it a good stir and add any desired toppings, such as fresh fruit, nuts, or granola.

Enjoy your simple and delicious chia pudding!
